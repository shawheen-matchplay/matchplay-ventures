Investing in outliers.
Backed with conviction.

MatchPlay Ventures is an early-stage investor focused on Life Sciences, FinTech, and B2B SaaS. We integrate deep sector expertise with a rigorous diligence process, backing companies with validated business models, strong leadership, and a path to scale.

Our mission is to identify and nurture early-stage outliers, founders driving real innovation, while generating consistent, long-term value for our investors. We bring conviction, capital, and a hands-on partnership model.

The Reasons
01

Outliers

We back the companies that break the pattern. AI rewriting how clinical trials run. Modern software entering industries that never had it. Breakthroughs that reset what a category costs. The advantage compounds because the rest of the field can't follow.

02

Conviction

Consensus is already priced in. Our diligence is built to find conviction, not crowd it. We pressure-test the founder, the wedge, and the path to scale. When the evidence holds, we don't hedge. We commit.

The Sectors

Life Sciences

Breakthrough therapeutics, medical devices, and digital health: technologies extending what's possible in human care.

FinTech

Solutions advancing financial access, transparency, and efficiency for consumers, businesses, and the rails between.

B2B SaaS

Data-driven platforms with recurring revenue, strong retention, and clear product-led pull from enterprise buyers.
